# Assignment (09/02/2026)

## Assignment Name

AI Around Me

## Description

List 10 real-life AI applications used in daily life and explain how Machine Learning works behind each in 1–2 lines.

---

### 1. Google Search

AI helps show the most relevant search results. Machine Learning learns from user searches and clicks to improve results.

### 2. YouTube Recommendations

AI suggests videos based on what we watch and like. ML studies our viewing history to recommend similar videos.

### 3. Google Maps

AI predicts traffic and suggests the fastest route. ML analyzes location data from many users to estimate traffic conditions.

### 4. Email Spam Filter

AI automatically moves spam emails to the spam folder. ML learns from examples of spam and normal emails.

### 5. Voice Assistants (Google Assistant, Siri)

AI understands voice commands and gives responses. ML is used for speech recognition and language understanding.

### 6. Netflix / OTT Recommendations

AI suggests movies and shows based on our watch history. ML analyzes user preferences and ratings.

### 7. Face Unlock in Smartphones

AI recognizes the user’s face to unlock the phone. ML compares facial features with stored face data.

### 8. Online Shopping (Amazon / Flipkart)

AI recommends products based on past searches and purchases. ML analyzes customer behavior and interests.

### 9. Keyboard Auto-Correct

AI corrects spelling and predicts the next word. ML learns from large text datasets and typing patterns.

### 10. Instagram / Facebook Feed

AI decides which posts appear first in the feed. ML analyzes likes, comments, and interactions to personalize the feed.

---

**Conclusion:**
AI is used in many daily applications to make services smarter and more personalized. Machine Learning helps these systems improve by learning from user data and behavior.
